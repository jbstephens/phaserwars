# Phaser Wars

A two-player family fighting game. Single HTML file, no build step, no external assets — everything (graphics, sounds, music, voices, app icon) is generated at runtime.

**Play it:** open `fightinggame.html` in any modern browser, or visit the [GitHub Pages site](https://jbstephens.github.io/phaserwars/).

## Roster

Ben, Luke, Mom, Dad — pick one each (or two of the same).

## Controls

| | Move | Jump | Attack | Switch weapon |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Player 1** | A / D | W | F | G |
| **Player 2** | ← / → | ↑ | / | . |

Tap jump twice for a flipping double-jump. **Pause / resume:** `Esc` or `P`. On iPad, on-screen D-pad and buttons appear at each end of the screen — kids hold one end each; the pause button sits in the top-right corner.

## Weapons (cycle with switch)

Lightsaber · Phaser · Laser · Grenade · Boomerang · Freeze Ray · Mine · Healing Potion · Forcefield · Shield

## Modes

- **Versus** — 2 humans
- **Arcade** — 1 human vs CPU ladder: 3 ramping-difficulty rounds, then a boss fight against **ZORG the alien**

## Stages

Space Station, City Rooftops, Volcano, Ice Planet, Jungle Canopy — each with jumpable platforms and (some) stage hazards (lava, falling icicles, bouncy mushrooms).

## Extras

- Mystery boxes drop random power-ups (invincibility, double damage, heal, speed, rare mech mode)
- Toggleable Meteor Mode (random meteors dodge-or-die)
- Combo counter + hit-pause + slow-mo K.O.s
- Synth music with 4 rotating tracks
- Persistent win counts saved per fighter (localStorage)
